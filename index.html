Who we are

Building Data Labs offers data-driven solutions for building owners, property managers, architects, and engineers to optimize projects. We help visualize building data using D models, web apps, AR, and VR, enabling real interaction to tackle management challenges. Our services include as-built modeling, BIM management, AR/VR models, and consulting in AutoDesk Revit. We also provide software solutions for data integration web development, and project management tools like SnapTools and SnapCRM, helping stakeholders achieve to 30% ROI on maintenance costs.
